# 10 · Track Selection + Idea Library

## The one question: what can we realistically prove by 5 PM?

| Track | Choose if | Your proof | Root param |
|-------|-----------|-----------|------------|
| **Virality** (164) | Team understands content/distribution; clear public sharing loop; access to an audience | People discovered, shared, visited, acted | Signups 25x |
| **Revenue** (208, hardest) | You know a painful problem + the buyer; product usable AND purchasable today; strong at sales | Real users signed up, used, **paid** | Signups 20x |
| **AI as Agency** (164) ✅ US | Strongest at engineering/agents/orchestration; can replace a COMPLETE human function; agents work across real tools | A working agent team completed a real job end to end | Real output 20x |

**Decision rule:** story people share → Virality · buyer + payment → Revenue · agents completing a full business function → AI as Agency.
Don't pick the most interesting track — pick the one with the clearest evidence by 5 PM.

⚠️ Track locked at registration. No switching. Cross-track wins still pay (half weight, 50 cap).
