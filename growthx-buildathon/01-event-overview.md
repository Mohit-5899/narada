# 01 · Event Overview

## Why GrowthX buildathons are different
- 🚀 **Everyone ships** — the whole day is built around getting to a working demo
- 👥 **The room** — founders, engineers, operators who ship; the room is half the value
- 🎯 **Build to win** — exact scoring parameters given before writing a line
- 🔥 **Intensity** — comes from the room; that's the point

## Schedule
| Time | Phase | What happens |
|------|-------|--------------|
| 10:00 AM | **Kickoff** | Context, rules, Hermes walkthrough. Pick track + idea |
| 11:00 AM | **Build** | 8-hour sprint. Solo or teams. Build with/on Hermes; back half = collecting proof |
| 5:30 PM | **Demo** | On stage, live: 2-min demo + 1-min proof + 1-min Q&A. Not recorded |

## What's at stake
- 🏆 1st: $10,000+ per team · 🥈 2nd: $5,800+ · 🥉 3rd: $3,000+
- 🎁 Every accepted builder: $400+ regardless

## Key takeaway
Back half of the sprint = **proof collection** (live dashboards, repeated runs, verified numbers). Build in the first 4–5 hours, prove for the rest.
