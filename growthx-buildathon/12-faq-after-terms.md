# 12 · FAQ, After & Terms

## FAQ — the answers that matter

**Format**
- Solo or team? Either; same rubric regardless of size.
- Switch tracks after registration? **No.** Cross-track bonus still pays for wins elsewhere.
- Pre-event code? Helper utilities yes; an existing product no. The scored thing gets built today.
- Must I use Hermes? Yes — coding partner (session receipts) or base harness (≥1 capability doing real work). Both allowed.

**Scoring**
- Points: L1–L5, points = (L−1) × weight. Overflow past L5 uncapped on flagged parameters.
- Why does scoring speed up past L5? On purpose — past the ceiling every point is verified evidence, and verified evidence keeps paying.
- Signup verification: live DB check (not screenshots), traffic cross-checked, customers called, emails bounce-tested.
- Anti-spoof (Virality): >1 visitor per 10 weighted impressions, or >1 signup per 2 visitors → parameter drops to L1. Both tripped → manual review.
- Revenue = money from selling the product, not your time. Removal test: kill the product, does revenue stop?
- Cross-track bonus: half weight, 50 cap, same evidence bar, nothing paid twice.

**Partners & tools**
- Power-ups: flat +25 each, no cap, real use only (mentor must see it). All six = +150.
- Dodo account alone = 0. A **live checkout in the product** counts.
- Own LLM keys / other AI assistants? Yes — the Hermes rule still must hold.

**Demo & edge cases**
- Demo fails on stage? Narrate what should happen, recover, move on. Recovery > perfection.
- Numbers after submission? Only what's verifiable at judging counts.
- Borderline starting point? Flag to a mentor early. Hiding origin = auto-DQ.

## After the event

**Tonight:** push code to GitHub · post the demo (tag @growthx.club, #HermesBuildathon) · write down what you learned about how you work.

**This week:** actually use the agent (it keeps running — that's the point of Hermes) · show the process at work · point it at something that matters (inbox, side project).

**If continuing:**
- Week 1: fix what's broken, no new features, real data, reliable for one user (you)
- Week 2: show 3 people who have the problem (not nice friends)
- Week 3: add the ONE feature two of the three asked for
- Then you'll know: project or product.

Submit to the GrowthX showcase either way — broken-but-interesting counts.

## Terms (the ones that bite)
- GrowthX can modify/cancel/disqualify at any time; judging final, no appeals
- **Your work, your IP** — GrowthX claims nothing on your build
- Handbook/rubric/idea library = GrowthX IP; Hermes marks = Nous Research
- Eligibility: registered, 18+, physically on the floor
- **Verification consent**: submitting = consenting to read-only analytics access, DB spot checks, contact checks with signups. Refusing verification zeroes that parameter
- Photo/video release: you're being recorded
- Back up your work — your laptop is your problem
